# LSEG-C-Training

## Workshop Schedule

| Week | Duration | Mode | Course Content |
|-----:|----------|------|----------------|
| 1 | 1 hour | Virtual (Zoom) | Introduction to the workshop<br>High-level company intro, our tech stack and the high performance computing we do<br>Why C++ is critical for High performance & mission critical applications and benefits of learning C++|
| 2 | 2 hours | Virtual (Zoom) | Basics of C++<br>Compiler process and optimization levels<br>Programming fundamentals (variables, functions, arrays, loops, if-else)<br>Data alignment and intro performance basics<br>Pointers and memory<br>Stack vs Heap and CPU interaction |
| 3 | 1.5 hours | Virtual (Zoom) | SOLID principles<br>Design patterns (Creational, Structural, Behavioral)<br>Additional patterns and anti patterns |
| 4 | 2 hours | Virtual (Zoom) | C++ Abstract Machine<br>Classes and class design guidelines<br>Virtual functions and dynamic_cast<br>Member initialization<br>Avoiding deep hierarchies<br>Modern C++ features<br>Move semantics<br>Smart pointers<br>Data structures (Arrays, Sets, Maps, Vectors)<br>STL and Boost<br>Iteration, insert/remove operations<br>Exception handling (try-catch) |
| 5 | 1.5 hours | Virtual (Zoom) | OOP concepts<br>Classes and objects<br>Header files<br>Access modifiers<br>Encapsulation, Abstraction, Inheritance, Polymorphism<br>Virtual functions and abstract classes |
| 6 | 2 hours | Virtual (Zoom) | Group project introduction<br>Evaluation criteria<br>Certificate details |
| 7 | 2 hours | Virtual (Zoom) | High Performance Computing concepts<br>Concurrent programming introduction<br>Read-write locks<br>Deadlocks<br>Concurrent modifications<br>Thread binding |
| 8–9 | Two weeks | Project | Student project development period |
| 10–11 | Two weeks | Evaluation | Project marking period |
| 12 | 4 hours | On-site (Malabe Office) | Company introduction and field visit<br>Project evaluation<br>Award and certificate ceremony |
